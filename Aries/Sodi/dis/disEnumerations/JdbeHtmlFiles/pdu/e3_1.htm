Nomenclature field

Size: 16
Data Type: enumerated
Description: This field shall specify the nomenclature for a particular communications device. Nomenclatures are a combination of letters and/or numbers arranged in a specific sequence to provide a short significant method of identification. They are derived from MIL-STD-196D. Commercial, Federal Communications Commission (FCC). Federal Aviation Administration (FAA). Department of Transportation (DOT) or foreign methods of identifying specific equipment, series and/or families of equipment. This field shall be represented by a 16-bit enumeration.
