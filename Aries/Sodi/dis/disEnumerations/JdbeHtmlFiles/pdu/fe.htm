Receiver State field

Size: 16
Data Type: enumerated
Description: This field shall indicate the state of the receiver, which shall either be idle or active. This field shall be represented by a 16-bit enumeration (see Section 9 in EBV-DOC).

Enumerations:

Value Description
0 Off
1 On but not receiving
2 On and receiving
