Transmit State field

Size: 8
Data Type: enumerated
Description: This field shall specify whether a radio is off, powered but not transmitting, or powered and transmitting. This field shall be represented by an 8-bit enumberation. (see Section 9 in EBV-DOC).

Enumerations:

Value Description
0 Off
1 On but not transmitting
2 On and transmitting
