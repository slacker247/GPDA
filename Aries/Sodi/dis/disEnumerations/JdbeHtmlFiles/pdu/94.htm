Repair Complete PDU

Size: 224
Description: When a Service Request PDU has been received and the repairing entity has completed a requested repair, the repairing entity shall notify the receiving entity of the repair by issuing a Repair Complete PDU. The Repair Complete PDU shall contain the following fields:

a) PDU Header - This field shall contain data common to all DIS PDUs. The PDU Header shall be represented by the PDU Header Record (see 5.3.24).
b) Receiving Entity Identification - This field shall identify the entity that is requesting repairs. This field shall be represented by an Entity Identifier Record (see 5.3.14).
c) Repairing Entity Identification - This field shall identify the repairing entity. This field shall be represented by an Entity Identifier Record (see 5.3.14).
d) Repair - This field shall describe the repair performed. This field shall be represented by a 16-bit enumeration Type (see 5.3.26 and Section 6 in EBV-DOC).
