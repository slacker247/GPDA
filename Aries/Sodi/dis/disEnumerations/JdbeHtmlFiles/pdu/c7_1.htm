Pulse Repetition Frequency (PRF) Field

Size: 32
Data Type: Float
Description: This field specifies the average PRF of the emission in hertz. PRF modulation for a particular emitter and mode shall be derived from database parameters storede in the receiving entity. This field shall be represented by a 32-bit floating point number.
