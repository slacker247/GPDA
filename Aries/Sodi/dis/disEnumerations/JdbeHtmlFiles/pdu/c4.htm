Frequency Field

Size: 32
Data Type: Float
Description: This field shall specify the center frequency of the emission in hertz. Frequency modulation for a particular emitter and mode shall be derived from database parameters strored in the receiving entity. This field shall be represented by a 32-bit floating point number.
