Elevation Beamwidth field

Size: 32
Data Type: float
Units: radians
Description: The full width of the beam to the -3dB power density points in the x-z plane of the beam coordinate system. Elevation Beamwidth shall be represented by a 32-bit floating point number in the units of radian.
