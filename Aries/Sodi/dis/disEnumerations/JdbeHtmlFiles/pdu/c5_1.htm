Frequency Range Field

Size: 32
Data Type: Float
Description: This field shall specify the bandwidth of the frequencies corresponding to the Frequency field. Thus, if, for operational purposes, the Frequency is supposed to be a single number, then the Frequency Range shall be zero. If the Frequency Range is non-zero, then it may indicate a range of agile RFs (unintentional or intentional) for a radar and it may indicate a 3 dB bandwidth for a jammer. This field shall be represented by a 32-bit floating point number.
