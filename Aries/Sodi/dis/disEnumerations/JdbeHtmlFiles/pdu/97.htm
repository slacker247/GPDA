Repair Result Field

Size: 8
Data Type: Enumerated
Description: This field shall specify the result of the repair specified in the Repair Complete PDU. This field shall be represented by an 8-bit enumeration (see Section 6 in EBV-DOC).

Enumerations:

Value Description
0 other
1 repair ended
2 invalid repair
3 repair interrupted
4 service canceled by the supplier
