Order field

Size: 8
Data Type: Unsigned
Description: Specifies the highest order of the expansion in spherical harmonics, counting from 0 (zero). There are 2n+1 coefficients for each order n, and a total of N**2 + 2N +1 coefficients through order N. Order shall by represented by a 32-bit unsigned integer.
